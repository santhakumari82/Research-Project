Advantages of Backtesting:
1. Assists investors in understanding the market: They may receive useful information from this that they can use to make other investing decisions.
2. There is no risk of financial losses: As a result, investors can test various investment theories without putting their own or their clients' money at risk.
Disadvantages of Backtesting:
1. It is challenging to include all charges: Calculating all expenditures that may have been included in prior trades might also be challenging.
2. Controlling every factor is challenging: This has the drawback that not all of the elements that go into making a good investment can be under your control.
