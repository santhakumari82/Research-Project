BackTesting of Cryptocurrency

What is backtesting? Backtesting is a method for determining whether a model's predictions match actual data. It is used for evaluating a trading strategy's probable performance by using sets of actual, historical data. You can select one technique over another based on the test results to achieve the best outcomes. With the help of backtesting, one can quickly and efficiently gather a vast amount of data and information about your plan.

Importance of Backtesting: Backtesting is important because it can assist traders in optimising and improving their methods, identifying any technical or theoretical shortcomings, and gaining confidence in their strategy before implementing it on live markets.
